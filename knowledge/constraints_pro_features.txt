The following is a list of all features exclusive to our [Pro plans](https://carrd.co/docs/pro/plans):

10 Sites Build and publish up to **10** sites from a single account.100 Sites Build and publish up to **100** sites from a single account.1000 Sites Build and publish up to **1000** sites from a single account.25 Sites Build and publish up to **25** sites from a single account.250 Sites Build and publish up to **250** sites from a single account.50 Sites Build and publish up to **50** sites from a single account.500 Sites Build and publish up to **500** sites from a single account.Advanced Forms Build custom forms that integrate with [Zapier](https://zapier.com/), [Make](https://www.make.com/), [n8n](https://n8n.io/) and [Airtable](https://www.airtable.com/), send data to URLs, seamlessly incorporate payments and more.Advanced Settings Modify advanced element settings like attributes, custom styles, and JS events.Bigger Sites Build sites with an unlimited number of elements.Canonical URL Explicitly set the canonical URL of your site.Custom Domain URLs Publish sites to any custom domains you own (with full SSL support via [Let's Encrypt](https://letsencrypt.org/)).Custom Meta Tags Add custom meta tags to each of your sites.Custom Templates Build and use your own custom templates.Download Sites Download the unminified HTML, CSS, JS, and images for any sites you build (note: excludes server-side code).Embeds Directly embed your own code (including `script` tags) as well as external content.Forms Add fully functional contact forms and signup forms (using [Mailchimp](https://mailchimp.com/), [Kit](https://kit.com/), [ActiveCampaign](https://www.activecampaign.com/), [EmailOctopus](https://emailoctopus.com/), and [many more](https://carrd.co/docs/forms/setting-up-a-signup-form)) to your sites.Google Analytics Add an optional [Google Analytics](http://www.google.com/analytics) tracking ID to each of your sites to track and report traffic.High Quality Images Publish sites with bigger, higher quality images and backgrounds.Large Images and Videos Upload and use images/GIFs up to 16MB and videos up to 64MB.Local Fonts Serve font assets directly from your site.No Branding Publish sites without the "Made with Carrd" branding.Password Protection Restrict access to a site using a password.Premium Templates Access premium starting point templates exclusive to Pro plans.Premium URLs Publish sites to unbranded URLs (like .crd.co) and punycode URLs.QR Codes Generate and download scannable QR codes for your sites.Redirects Moving sites to Carrd? Preserve your existing links by redirecting them to sections or external URLs.Share Images Show custom images (instead of screenshots) when sharing your sites on social media.Sharing Share access to your sites with other Carrd users.Site Files Customize various supporting site files (such as robots.txt and ads.txt).Site Icons Show custom icons (aka "favicons") in the address bar when visiting your sites.Site Transfers Transfer ownership of sites to other Carrd users.Slideshows Create fully animated slideshows as backgrounds or standalone elements.Update Frequency Explicitly set the update frequency of a site to help improve its performance.Variables Insert dynamic content into supported elements.Video Uploads Upload and use your own videos throughout your site (or as its background).Widgets Embed third-party widgets from [Stripe](https://stripe.com/checkout), [PayPal](https://paypal.com/), [Gumroad](https://gumroad.com/), [Typeform](https://www.typeform.com/), and [Facebook](https://facebook.com/).
