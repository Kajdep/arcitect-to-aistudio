Sophisticated Sans Serif Fonts in Carrd
---------------------------------------

Most **sans style fonts** (Carrd fonts included) are built from less intricate shapes compared to serif type designs. This gives them a cleaner, less playful and more technical look. This cool on-top-of-things attitude can bring stylishness and elegance to your site layout if used right. In our collection of sans serif fonts for Carrd we looked for **sleek and simple** letters and a **well balanced** overall appearance. Check out all sans serif font in our [Carrd font chooser](https://zite.design/carrd-fonts/).
